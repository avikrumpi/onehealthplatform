Health Guidelines

Essential Women's Health Information
üåü

Essential Health Practices

  • Regular health screenings and check-ups
  • Maintain a balanced diet rich in nutrients
  • Regular physical activity (30 minutes daily)
  • Stress management and mental wellness
  • Adequate sleep (7-9 hours)
  • Stay hydrated
üî¨

Screening Guidelines

  • Breast Cancer: Annual mammogram after age 40
  • Cervical Cancer: Pap test every 3 years (21-65 years)
  • Bone Health: DEXA scan after menopause
  • Heart Health: Blood pressure and cholesterol checks
  • Diabetes: Regular blood sugar monitoring
üõ°Ô∏è

Preventive Measures

  • HPV vaccination for cervical cancer prevention
  • Regular self-breast examination
  • Maintaining healthy weight
  • Limiting alcohol consumption
  • Avoiding smoking
  • Safe sun exposure with SPF protection
